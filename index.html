Who we are

Workshop & Wireframe is a small, hands-on digital studio obsessed with one thing: making your business sound like itself online.

We're not a big agency and we're not a "fast AI website" generator. We're the in-between: a focused little team that actually listens, asks questions, and then builds your website, chatbot and basic social presence around your real personality, not around a template.

When you work with W&W, you get people who care about the story behind your business – why you started, who you serve, how you talk when you're relaxed – and we turn that into a clear, confident online presence.

What we do

At W&W, we specialise in personality-driven online presence for small businesses, creators and service providers.

We help you with:

  • Personality & brand voice workshops – simple, guided questions that pull your story, tone and "way of speaking" out of your head and into a usable voice guide.
  • Websites that sound like you – clean, responsive sites that don't fall apart when you open the dev console and don't read like copy-paste AI.
  • Chatbots that don't feel like robots – focused, friendly chat flows that answer FAQs, capture leads or help with bookings, written in your style.
  • Basic social media presence – profile clean-ups, bio and about sections, and a starter batch of caption ideas that match your new voice.
  • Fixing broken DIY / AI sites – taking what you already have and rebuilding it into something stable, fast and human-sounding.

The end result is simple: when someone finds you online, they meet the real you, not a generic template.

How we work

We keep the process simple and human:

  1. Workshop – We sit down (online or in person) and ask the right questions about your business, your customers and your voice. No jargon, just conversation.
  2. Wireframe – We map out what your website and chatbot should say and where everything should live, so the structure fits your story and your goals.
  3. Build & refine – We design, develop and test the site and chatbot, then refine it with your feedback until it sounds and feels like you.

From there, you can plug into one of our packages for ongoing tweaks, content and support – so your online presence can grow as your business grows.
